About Us

Help application is redefining the scope of service provision in Nigeria and beyond. We are on-demand platform that connects clients with service providers. Whether you need a carpenter, or a house cleaner or a delivery person, Help app has you covered. We make it easy to find and book those services that you need and we pride ourselves on our reliability and convenience. We have professional service providers that has been vetted and rated across many industries waiting to accept your bookings and deliver the best of services to you. Simple download the help app , select the service that you wish to receive, relax and allow our professionals take it up from there.

To provide a comprehensive, convenient and easy to use platform for clients and service providers to find, book and deliver services

To create a fair and transparent marketplace where service providers are fairly compensated and clients get the best possible service

To make skill acquisition and service rendering the go-to which in turns creates a positive impact on the society and in turn increases productivity
